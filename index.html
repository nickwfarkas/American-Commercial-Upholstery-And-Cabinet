The Artist Spotlight

Stephen Farkas
Founder & Artist
Stephen Farkas started with a dream to create high quality carpentry solutions at a cost that restaurateurs and small businesses owners can afford. This dream could not be fulfilled without branching off and starting his own business. With his over 40 years of experience, Steve has stream lined the carpentry process and created strong relationships with suppliers of the highest quality materials, passing those savings off to his valued customers. A small business entrepreneur at heart, Steve has had the pleasure of working with some of the most influential businesses in the Detroit area.

Paul Zanotti
Artist
An artist at heart, Paul Zanotti, is highly skilled about everything that American Commercial Upholstery and Cabinets offers. A veteran member of the team, Paul takes leads on many creations. When Steve had a battle with Leukemia, Paul stepped in along with others in the American crew during a pandemic when restaurants were truly struggling, to lead the team through the turbulent times.
